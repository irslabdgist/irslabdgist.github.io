I am an Assistant Professor at Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, [DGIST](https://www.dgist.ac.kr/eng/index.do){:target="_blank"}, Daegu, Korea, since 2024. 

From 2023 to 2024, I was a Postdoctoral Scholar in [Department of Electrical Engineering](https://ee.stanford.edu/){:target="_blank"} (advised by Prof. [Amin Arbabian](https://arbabianlab.stanford.edu/){:target="_blank"}) at [Stanford University](https://www.stanford.edu/){:target="_blank"}, Stanford, CA, US. I received the M.S. and Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from [POSTECH](https://postech.ac.kr/eng/){:target="_blank"} (advised by Prof. [Kyung-Tae Kim](http://iras.postech.ac.kr/main/index.php){:target="_blank"}), Pohang, Korea, in 2019 and 2023, respectively. I completed the B.S. degree in Computer Science from [Korea University](https://www.korea.edu/sites/en/index.do){:target="_blank"}, Seoul, Korea, in 2017.

### Research Interests:
My main research focuses on 
* **Radio+X Perception Technologies**: wireless indoor perception, wireless 3D human understanding, wireless health monitoring, radio+X sensor fusion
* **AI for Wireless/Radar**: wireless-centric learning, data-efficient learning, complex ML
* **Defense/Remote Sensing**: SAR, drone detection
